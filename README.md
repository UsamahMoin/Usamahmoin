## What I build
I build courses like real products: clear goals, tight feedback loops, and work that ships.  
My technical lane is practical and systems-first: **APIs, databases, pipelines, and cloud deployments** with reproducible setups and clean documentation.  
On the research side, I’m interested in **auditory scene analysis** and robust feature representations.

## How I teach
I’m not trying to keep students busy. I’m trying to make them capable.

- **Ship weekly** (small deliverables that compound)
- **Explain your code** (demos + walkthroughs + reasoning)
- **Build habits** (Git discipline, docs, basic tests, respectful debugging)
- **Own your tools** (if AI is used: disclose what it did, what changed, what you understand)

**Grading:** reward clarity/correctness/growth. Penalize copy-paste and “it works on my machine.”
